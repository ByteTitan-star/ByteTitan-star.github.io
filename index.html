📄 Publications

Selection Aware Poisoning Boosting Clean Label Backdoor Attacks via Distribution Deviation and Projection Residual Metrics
Author A, Wang Xin, Author C
[Accepted at IJCNN 2026]
For clean-label backdoor attacks, we propose two training-free, auxiliary-data-free sample selection strategies: Distribution Deviation Metric (DDM) and Projection Residual Metric (PRM). They precisely identify high-risk samples from a global statistical perspective, significantly improve attack success rates across diverse datasets and architectures, and effectively evade existing defenses.
